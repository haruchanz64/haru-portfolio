About Me

I am a graduate with a Bachelor of Science in Entertainment and Multimedia Computing, specialized in Game Development, from the University of Caloocan City.

My expertise spans the full development pipeline, from designing engaging gameplay mechanics to implementing scalable backend systems and developer tools. I specialize in Unity and C#, with additional experience in engines like Godot. My portfolio includes projects ranging from educational games to custom Unity editor tools, mobile apps, and automation scripts.

Beyond game development, I explore mobile development to broaden my technical reach, leveraging my knowledge in multiple programming languages, databases, and frameworks. I am driven by the challenge of solving complex problems and transforming creative concepts into interactive, polished experiences.

I am on the lookout for potential work opportunities where I can contribute my skills and passion for mobile and game development. If you are interested in collaborating, have a project in mind or work opportunities, feel free to reach out!
