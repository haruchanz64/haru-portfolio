About Me

I am a systems-oriented developer focused on the architecture and logic that power complex applications. My work bridges the gap between high-level game engines (Unity, Godot) and robust backend systems utilizing C# (.NET) and Java (Spring Boot).

I specialize in building the "heavy-duty" infrastructure—custom engine tooling, workflow automation, and scalable server-side logic. My philosophy is rooted in clean, efficient code, and I am currently exploring lower-level game development through Bevy and Raylib.

I am available for freelance work involving backend API development, custom engine tools, and C#/.NET optimization.
