Background

The curiosity about how video games were made started to fill my mind in 2015 and continued to grow over the following years. During my senior year of high school, we had a major subject that covered C# using the .NET Framework, which is very similar to Java, the language I'm currently learning.

This sparked my interest in C# and led me to discover the Unity game engine in December 2018. While learning Unity, I created small game prototypes to experiment and build my skills. However, I didn't backed up all of my prototype games I made back then, so I can only showcase some of my most recent projects.

In 2021, I saw a local university near me that offered a Bachelor of Science in Entertainment and Multimedia Computing. with a specialization in game development. I was fortunate to gain admission to this program and am now a junior college year-level BS Entertainment and Multimedia Computing student at the University of Caloocan City.
